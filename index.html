Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Aarti Sangrah is designed to respect your privacy and keep things simple: your data stays on your device.

Collection No personal data
Tracking No ads or trackers
Storage On-device only
Connection Internet only for sync
01

No Personal Data Collected

Aarti Sangrah does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or location. No account registration is required.

02

All Data Stays on Your Device

Aartis, your favorites, and app preferences such as theme, font size, and language are stored locally on your device using SQLite. This data never leaves your phone and is not accessible to us.

03

Internet Used Only for Content Sync

The app connects to the internet solely to download the latest aarti content from our CDN via jsDelivr. No usage data, analytics, or personal information is sent during this process. The app works fully offline once synced.

04

No Ads, No Trackers

Aarti Sangrah contains no advertisements and uses no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. Your reading habits and preferences are never monitored or shared.

05

Safe for All Ages

Aarti Sangrah is a devotional app suitable for all ages. Since we collect no personal data, the app is safe for children to use.

06

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page. Continued use of the app after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
